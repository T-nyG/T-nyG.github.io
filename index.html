About Me

Tony Garcés is a 23-year-old food service worker interested in learning how to code. He graduated from the Marc and Eva Stern Math and Science School in 2014 with honors and proceeded to San Jose University where he studied Computer Engineering for a year, before returning home to Santa Monica. Still trying to make an effort to learn, he applied to the Santa Monica College graphic design program, while working two jobs, where he spent another year before having to drop his courses in order to take care of his household. Many years later, his interest in challenging himself led him to find the Coding Bootcamp offered through UCLA Extension, where he finds himself learning and having fun once again in a competitive environment.
